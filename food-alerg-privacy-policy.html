Privacy Policy Food Allergy Tracker app. Last updated: February 24, 2025. This policy describes what data the Food Allergy Tracker app ("the App," "we") collects and how it is used. 1. Data We Process - Account data — user identifier, email address (if you sign in with an account). - Health data — allergies, intolerances, symptoms, and meals you enter. This data is stored locally on your device and/or in your account. - Food photos — images you attach to meal entries (when using the camera or photo gallery). - Technical data — device type, OS version, and language, used to ensure the App functions correctly. 2. Purposes of Processing - Providing App features: food diary management, symptom and allergen tracking. - Improving the App and fixing bugs. - Complying with legal requirements where applicable. 3. Data Storage and Transfer Health data and photos are stored on your device by default. If you use cloud sync or an account, data may be transferred to our servers or hosting services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. 4. Device Permissions The App may request access to your camera and photo gallery to attach photos to meal entries. These permissions are used only for their stated purpose and can be revoked in your device settings at any time. 5. Your Rights You may delete your data at any time through the App's settings or by uninstalling the App. If you have an account, you may request an export or deletion of your data by contacting us using the details provided in the App. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via the contact information you have provided. 7. Contact For questions about personal data processing and privacy, please contact us using the details on the App's store page or in the App's Settings section.
